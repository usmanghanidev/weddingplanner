Who We Are

Crafting Timeless
Pakistani Celebrations

"Every Pakistani wedding is a tapestry of love, culture, and heritage — we weave it into unforgettable memories."

Shadi Mahal has been Pakistan's most trusted wedding planning company for over 15 years. From the bustling lanes of Lahore to the majestic halls of Karachi, we have orchestrated hundreds of dream weddings that honour the rich traditions of Pakistani culture.

Our team of dedicated professionals understands every ceremony — Mayun, Mehndi, Nikkah, Barat, and Walima — ensuring every detail reflects your family's unique heritage and vision.

  • Full-service luxury wedding coordination
  • Authentic Pakistani decor & floral artistry
  • Premium photography & videography
  • Traditional cuisine & catering management
  • Live music, qawwali & entertainment
